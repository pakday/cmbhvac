FAQs

Got Questions? We’ve Got Straight Answers.
At CMB Plumbing, we believe peace of mind starts with clarity. Whether you’re dealing with a leaky faucet, wondering about water heater options, or curious about our pricing and service times, this FAQ section is designed to give you quick, honest answers—so you know exactly what to expect before we even knock on your door.

Do you offer financing?

Of course we do! We work with many different banks and will help find you the right financing to fit your budget and credit score. Some of our most popular plans are 0% financing for 36-60 months.

Do you offer weekend appointments?

Absolutely! We understand that everyone’s schedule is a little different and we are happy to arrange a time that fits your schedule. Give us a ring and we’ll be happy to accommodate you!

How fast can I have a repair completed?

Most new plumbing installations can be scheduled and installed the next day after your in-home measurement. Equipment shortages, emergency repairs and specialized components can vary. Your exact installation date may vary due to supply chain factors out of our control.

Are permits and inspection fees included?

Permits and inspections fees may be required depending on your location and not included on this estimate. Each town and county are a little different, simply ask your comfort designer which permits are required so they can add them to you package.

Do you offer any other brands than the equipment listed?

Yep, we sure do! We have access to many others. Give us a ring and we’d be happy to find the perfect plumbing for your home!
